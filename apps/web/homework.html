How This Works

Below you'll find every section of the Aeros website — both the Main Page and the About Page — with a picture showing exactly which part of the site we're talking about.

For each section, you'll see what it does for the reader, our assessment of what's working and what isn't, and then your homework — specific questions and prompts for you to answer.

Don't try to write marketing copy. Just answer the questions the way you'd explain things to a colleague. We'll take your raw answers and turn them into polished website text that sounds like you.

Voice memos are great. If writing feels like a chore, open the voice recorder on your phone, read the question, and just talk through your answer. Send us the audio — it works extremely well.

Each section is marked with a priority: HIGH means we're blocked without your input, MEDIUM means it significantly improves the site, and LOWER means it can wait for a second pass.

Write your answer here...

Write your answer here...
